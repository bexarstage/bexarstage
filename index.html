Bexar Stage – Improv Comedy in San Antonio

For more information about Bexar Stage, check out the shows/events on our Facebook page. Email Tina with questions re: space rentals or corporate training/team-building.

Opened in May 2017, Bexar Stage quickly became San Antonio's premier venue for comedy entertainment and training, offering shows Thursday, Friday, and Saturday nights. Their free weekly show, Key Party, is now one of San Antonio's most attended events.

Improv is the art of creating theatre based on a simple suggestion from the audience. Everything you see on stage will be completely made up on the spot, by the performers. Because improv shows are entirely unique unto the moment, you’ll never see the same show twice!

Featuring a 6-level improv training program, as well as sketch and stand-up classes taught by industry professionals, as well as a variety of workshops, Bexar Stage's Training Center specializes in finding the funny in everyone.

Centrally located in the Pearl District of downtown San Antonio, Bexar Stage is the perfect BYOB spot to catch a show after dinner.

Runner up Best Comedy Club in San Antonio Current’s Best of SA: Reader’s Choice Poll 2018. (Honorable Mention Best Community Theatre and Best Place to Bring Your Side-Piece/Tinder Date.)
