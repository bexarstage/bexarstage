Few Words About Us

Bexar Stage is San Antonio's premier venue for comedy entertainment and training, offering shows Thursday, Friday, and Saturday nights. The free weekly show, Key Party, is now one of San Antonio's most attended events.

If you're looking for cool stuff to do in San Antonio, Bexar Stage is the perfect BYOB spot to catch a show after dinner.

What is Improv?

Improv is the art of creating theatre based on a simple suggestion from the audience. Everything you see on stage will be completely made up on the spot, by the performers. Because improv shows are entirely unique unto the moment, you’ll never see the same show twice!

Improv Comedy Classes

Featuring a 6-level improv training program, as well as sketch and stand-up classes taught by industry professionals, as well as a variety of workshops, Bexar Stage's Training Center specializes in finding the funny in everyone.
